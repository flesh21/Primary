Paulina Kaminska - interior decoration is like a storytelling.


With a commitment to a narrative connection, her work sets the stage for her clients’ lives
with understated flair and personal expression in a modern eclectic way.

Armed with a MFA from Art Center College of Design, her work pays homage to timeless classics, modern forms,
and geometric themes. The process is collaborative, yet focused to a single vision.

Paulina’s creative portfolio also includes directing and producing a short film based on her own screenplay
and appearing as an on-screen interior designer for the DIY Network's show

Paulina lives and works in Los Angeles, and she'd appreciate it if you would please use a coaster.
